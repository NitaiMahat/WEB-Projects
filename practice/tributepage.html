The Man Who Danced with the Ball and Captivated the World

TRIBUTE INFO

  • 1960 – Born on October 30 in Villa Fiorito, Buenos Aires, Argentina.
  • 1976 – Makes his professional debut for Argentinos Juniors just before his 16th birthday.
  • 1979 – Leads Argentina to victory in the FIFA U-20 World Cup, winning the Golden Ball as the tournament’s best player.
  • 1986 – Captains Argentina to World Cup glory in Mexico, scoring the famous "Hand of God" goal
  • 2019 – Becomes head coach of Gimnasia de La Plata in Argentina.
  • 2020 – Passes away on November 25 due to a heart attack at the age of 60.

"Maradona was a player of dreams, capable of feats beyond imagination. His passion and genius on the pitch made him eternal." — FIFA
